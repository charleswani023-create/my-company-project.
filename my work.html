ASWAN COOPERATIVE COMPANY (ACC) started in 2014 as a small cooperative society with a least membership of about eight members.

It started growing rapidly in in 2020 due to the influence of christian missionaries who invested a lot to company and also other foreign investors.

The project is currently managed by a great south sudanese Dr.Eng CHARLES WANI SAM who advocated for the involement of foreign investors in the company

Currently we can employ over 200 people of various capabiliyies annually because of the rapid growth of the company

HOW TO APPLY

Applicants should follow the following guideline while apply for either a program(student) or applying for a job(employment seekers)

STUDENTS

  1. Go to our main app and click on our website that is https://www.aswan.ac.uc
  2. Click on sign up if you are still new or sign in if you already have an account
  3. Enter your correct names as used in the academic transcripts
  4. Enter your correct email address
  5. Enter your password
  6. Click on create account
  7. Fill the form correctly with the necessary details
  8. Chose at least three programs and the first choice is the priority
  9. Attached a single pdf copy of all your academic documents.
  10. Put an application fee of 200,000 by choosing the payment methods as indicated in the application form
  11. Finally click on submit form and the form is submited
  12. An email will be sent to the email address you have provided to confirm your apllicato status

EMPLOYMENT SEEKERS

  1. Click on our website that is https://www.aswan.ac.ug
  2. Click on jobs
  3. Select the job you wanted to apply for
  4. correctly provide your details to the form provided
  5. Attach a single pdf copy of all the necessary documents.
  6. Click on submit form
  7. An email will be sent to the email address you have provided notfying you for interviews incase you are shortlisted
  8. Check our shortlist on the website to know your success incase you fail to receive an email
